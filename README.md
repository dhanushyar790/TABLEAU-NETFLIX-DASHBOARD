**🎬 Netflix Dashboard**
**📊 Overview**
This project presents a data visualization dashboard built to analyze Netflix’s content library. It provides insights into the distribution of movies and TV shows, release trends, ratings, genres, and country-wise content availability. The dashboard helps users understand Netflix’s catalog composition and global reach.

**🧩 Key Features**
--> Total Movies vs TV Shows:  
  Compare the number of movies and TV shows available on Netflix.
--> Release Year Trend:  
  Visualize how Netflix content has evolved over time.
--> Top Ratings:  
  Identify the most frequent content ratings (e.g., TV-14, TV-MA).
--> Top Genres:  
  Explore popular genres like Drama, Documentary, Comedy, and Kids’ TV.
--> Content by Country:  
  See which countries contribute most to Netflix’s catalog.
--> TV Show and Movie Distribution:  
  Understand the proportion of movies vs TV shows.
--> Show Count by Date Added:  
  Track when new content was added to the platform.

**🛠️ Tools & Technologies**
Data Source --> netflix_titles.csv
Visualization --> Tableau
Design --> Color-coded charts and maps for clarity

**📈 Insights**
--> Netflix hosts over 6,000 movies and around 2,600 TV shows.
--> TV-MA and TV-14 are the most common ratings.
--> Dramas and Documentaries dominate the genre list.
--> The United States and India are among the top content-producing countries.
--> Content releases peaked around 2020, showing Netflix’s rapid expansion.

**🚀 How to Use**
--> Load the dataset (netflix_titles.csv) into Tableau or Power BI.
--> Connect data fields to the respective charts.
--> Explore interactive filters for year, genre, and country.
--> Gain insights into Netflix’s global content strategy.
